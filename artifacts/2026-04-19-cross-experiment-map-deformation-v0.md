# Cross-Experiment Map Deformation v0

Date: `2026-04-19`

Status: public-safe comparison summary, not claim validation.

## Experiments

- Experiment 001: `The Great Awakening of Human Consciousness`
- Experiment 002: `The neuroscience of mindfulness meditation`

## Core Result

The contrast source deforms the geometry inward.

Experiment 001 carried a large outer speculative band. Experiment 002 introduced
review-supported and methodological-caution claims near the evidential center,
plus a mid-radius limited-evidence band.

## Interpretation

This is the first sign that the geometry is not merely decorating the source.
When the source type changes, the map changes:

- mystical/symbolic source: outward speculative mass plus developmental motion
- conventional review source: inward caution/support cluster plus limited-evidence band
