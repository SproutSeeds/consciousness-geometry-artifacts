## Boundary

This artifact summarizes and maps public source material. It does not validate
the source claims.

## Synthesis

The source argues that consciousness is not merely a brain-local phenomenon but
a fundamental feature of reality. Its central organizing theme is connection:
people, Earth, cosmos, energy, awareness, spiritual traditions, symbolic
geometry, and personal transformation are presented as parts of one larger
pattern.

The source is strongest as a symbolic integration narrative. It offers a
coherent mythic frame around unity, inner work, ego balance, meditation, and
relational influence.

The source is weakest where it uses scientific language to support claims that
would require independent evidence, replication, or controls. This includes
claims around telepathy, channeling, scalar energy, Atlantis, base-12
suppression, and DNA activation.

Use a spiral to represent the source's recurring developmental sequence:

- encounter crisis or limitation
- turn inward
- balance ego
- practice attention
- reconnect with others and world
- expand identity into a wider frame

### Torus Exchange Model

Use a torus for source-energy language:

- inward flow: attention, insight, grounding
- outward flow: creativity, care, communication, influence
- circulation: relationship between self-transformation and collective change

moving, layered structure that can preserve evidence boundaries while allowing
